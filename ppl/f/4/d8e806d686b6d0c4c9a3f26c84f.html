JC REPUBLICAN, 01 JUN 1900, Brown's Creek items, pg 5: Mr. Samuel Atkins passed his 87th birthday last Sunday and his daughter, Mrs. Nees, made a surprise dinner for him and invited several of his children and grandchildren to come and help him enjoy it.

JC REPUBLICAN, 30 MAY 1902, Brown's Creek items, pg 5: Some of Samuel Atkins' relatives made a surprise dinner for him Tuesday of this week, it being his 89th birthday anniversary.

JC REPUBLICAN, 11 JUL 1902, Brown's Creek items, pg 5: Samuel Atkins has a swelling in his face that nearly closes his eyes.

JC REPUBLICAN, 27 FEB 1903, Brown's Creek items, pg 5: Mrs. Alta Tucker was down from Cawker City, last Friday, to see her grandfather, Sam'l Atkins. His son David and wife were down from Lebanon, Saturday and Sunday. The old gentleman does not gain strength very fast and he has said this would be his last sickness.
